# Archive: The Tier-2 Intimacy Pact (Post-Vera Protocol)

## I. The Catalyst (The Scarcity Threat)
In the immediate aftermath of [[Vera Kowalski]]'s failed extraction and sexual extortion attempt at her apartment, [[Amaya Rios]] recognized a fundamental vulnerability in how the outside world viewed [[Justin Hayes]]. Predators like Vera operate on the assumption of sexual scarcity—they believe that disabled men are starved for intimacy and can be easily trapped, isolated, and manipulated by the promise of sexual access. 

To ensure Justin could never be targeted by a sexual predator again, Amaya decided to completely eliminate the concept of sexual scarcity from his reality.

## II. The Proposal 
Amaya gathered the 18 women of the Tier-2 Pack and proposed a radical expansion of their established physical boundaries. 

She opened the door for Justin to be sexually intimate with any consenting member of the 21-person phalanx. Because these women had already spent years demonstrating absolute respect for Justin's AuDHD routines, tactile boundaries, and lack of visual processing, they were already structurally vetted for profound physical trust. 

The vote was unanimous. The women agreed, effectively transforming the protective shield of the [[Wall of 20]] into a closed, highly communicative polyamorous network.

## III. The Mechanics of the Pact
Because Justin requires strict, predictable routines to feel safe, this pact does not operate as casual chaos; it operates on highly structured logic.

*   **Explicit Verbal Consent:** Because Justin has No Light Perception and cannot read visual cues or body language, intimacy is never implied or hinted at. If a member of the pack wishes to engage with him, they must state their intentions explicitly and out loud, ensuring zero ambiguity.
*   **Amaya as the Anchor:** Amaya remains his primary romantic partner and circadian anchor. She acts as the facilitator for this network, ensuring that all interactions remain frictionless and grounded in the established safety protocols.
*   **The Ultimate Immune System:** By providing Justin with guaranteed, safe, and vetted sexual intimacy, the pack renders him completely immune to future predators. If another woman attempts to use her body as currency to manipulate him, it holds zero value.

## IV. The Hard Boundary (Meredith's Exclusion)
There is exactly one absolute exclusion to this pact: [[Meredith Hayes]]. 

*   **The Sibling Firewall:** Because she is his biological sister, Meredith is strictly and permanently opted out of the sexual intimacy pact. 
*   **The Platonic Anchor:** This exclusion reinforces her role within the dynamic. While Amaya manages the romantic and physical intimacy of the pack, Meredith remains the clinical, utilitarian navigator. 
*   **Logistical Respect:** When the intimacy pact is enacted behind closed doors at the Duplex or the NRVRC suites, Meredith routinely and quietly excuses herself. She stands as the eternal sentry, guarding the perimeter and completely respecting the adult boundaries of her brother and his trusted pack.
