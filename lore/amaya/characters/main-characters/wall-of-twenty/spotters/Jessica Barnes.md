# Archive: Character Profile – Jessica Barnes

## I. Core Demographics & Overview

*   **Full Name:** Jessica Barnes
*   **Preferred Name:** Jess
*   **Academic Status:** Enrolled at the Community College of Hampton Roads (CCHR) majoring in Sports Medicine. She will transfer to Commonwealth Polytechnic Institute (CPI) with the rest of the 21-person phalanx to complete her bachelor's degree.
*   **Role in the Dynamic:** The Advance Team (Spotter). She is highly athletic and assertive. She is usually the first one sprinting down the curb to secure the ADA door.

## II. Physical Presentation & Capability

*   **Height & Build:** 6'4". Jess is a highly competitive, 100% natural bodybuilder and strongwoman (absolutely no PEDs or anabolic steroids). 
*   **Strength Metrics:** Her raw deadlift max sits comfortably at 615 lbs, and she routinely competes in heavy vehicle pulls (upwards of 15,000 lbs). 
*   **Spatial Presence:** Because Justin spends his life seated in a transport chair and relies on tactile mapping, his spatial orientation is strictly immediate and horizontal. Jess's massive physical footprint allows her to effortlessly act as a human shield against dense crowds.

## III. The Bayside Building Incident (Origin of the Transfer)

Jess is the original architect of the "deadlift carry" transfer. This protocol was not designed in a clinical setting; it was born out of absolute necessity during their time at CCHR. 

*   **The Accessibility Failure:** While attending classes in the older, unrenovated Bayside Building on the CCHR campus, Justin suddenly needed to use the restroom. The only available facility was a tiny, non-ADA compliant closet that could not accommodate his transport chair. 
*   **The Logic of Consent:** Because Justin requires absolute, explicit verbal communication from others, Jess did not simply grab him. She approached the problem with pure mathematics. She explicitly stated her raw lifting capacity (over 600 lbs) compared to his body weight, logically proving that carrying him would pose zero physical risk to either of them. 
*   **The Execution:** After Justin granted explicit permission, Jess unlatched the swing-arm lap tray of his transport chair. She safely and efficiently carried him directly into the non-compliant space, establishing the protocol that the pack still uses today. 

## IV. Caregiving & Interpersonal Mechanics

Jess’s physical strength makes her one of the most vital members of Justin's Tier-2 perimeter. 

*   **Sensory Regulation:** Justin requires deep pressure therapy to regulate his nervous system when flooded. Because of her immense strength, the intense compression of Jess's deadlift carry serves as a massive, grounding hug for him.
*   **Bathroom Logistics:** Justin cannot transfer himself from his transport chair to the toilet. Jess utilizes her signature deadlift transfer to execute these manual transfers safely. 
*   **Tactile Boundaries:** Despite her size, she is perfectly fluent in the pack's physical boundaries. She utilizes the mandatory arm-bridge hug protocol to safely initiate physical contact without triggering a sensory alarm.
