# Archive: Character Profile – Jasmine Washington

## I. Core Demographics & Overview

*   **Full Name:** Jasmine Washington
*   **Academic Status:** Enrolled at the Community College of Hampton Roads (CCHR) majoring in Computer Science with a focus on Systems Architecture. She will transfer to Commonwealth Polytechnic Institute (CPI) with the rest of the 21-person phalanx to complete her bachelor's degree.
*   **Role in the Dynamic:** The Advance Team (Spotter). She is hyper-observant and excellent at reading transit signage and schedules from a distance.

## II. Physical Presentation & Capability

*   **Height & Build:** 5'5". Jasmine has a compact, agile frame. In contrast to the imposing physical size of Jess and Ashley, she is built for speed and maneuverability through dense urban spaces.
*   **Visual Acuity:** She possesses 20/10 vision. She acts as the long-range optical sensor for the pack, capable of discerning fine text, scrolling marquees, and bus numbers before the vehicles even enter the loading zone.

## III. Navigational Mechanics

Jasmine provides the critical navigational data that dictates how the physical advance team deploys.

*   **Transit Duty:** During transit chaos, she deploys ahead of the group to identify the correct bus. Because she is highly observant, she can read the schedules and route numbers from a distance, relaying the data back to the pack so Jess and Ashley know exactly which door to secure.
*   **The Agile Scout:** While Ashley parts the crowd, Jasmine slips through the gaps, ensuring the physical path is visually clear of sudden obstacles before the transport chair advances.

## IV. Caregiving & Interpersonal Mechanics

Jasmine bridges the gap between Justin's visual void and his digital reality.

*   **The Tech Synergy:** Because Justin strictly rejects bloated software environments and relies on raw, efficient text processing, Jasmine’s Systems Architecture background is vital. She prefers minimalist, native file routing and actively helps configure clean, POSIX-compliant scripts that feed the transit schedule data directly into his QBraille XL interface.
*   **Tactile Boundaries:** She is deeply respectful of Justin’s AuDHD routines. Like all 18 women in the Tier-2 pack, she provides him with genuine, platonic love and affection. She is entirely fluent in utilizing the mandatory arm-bridge hug protocol to safely initiate physical contact.
