# Archive: Character Profile – Ashley Williams

## I. Core Demographics & Overview

*   **Full Name:** Ashley Williams
*   **Academic Status:** Enrolled at the Community College of Hampton Roads (CCHR) majoring in Sports Medicine. She will transfer to Commonwealth Polytechnic Institute (CPI) with the rest of the 21-person phalanx to complete her bachelor's degree.
*   **Role in the Dynamic:** The Advance Team (Spotter). She is Jess’s counterpart on the advance team, known for her loud, projecting voice to hold the line against crowds.

## II. Physical Presentation & Capability

*   **Height & Build:** 6'1". Ashley is a competitive natural bodybuilder with a strict focus on muscular symmetry, breath control, and kinetic precision. 
*   **The Auditory Presence:** Ashley possesses an incredibly powerful diaphragm. She doesn't just yell; she projects her voice with theatrical, booming resonance. She acts as a polite but overwhelming auditory force that naturally compels crowds to part.

## III. Navigational Mechanics

Ashley operates alongside [[Jessica Barnes|Jess]] at the absolute front of the transit formation. 

*   **Transit Duty:** During transit chaos, she deploys ahead of the group to identify the correct bus and aggressively lock down the boarding area.
*   **Polite Crowd Control:** While Jess secures the physical ADA door, Ashley manages the human element. She utilizes her booming voice to cheerfully but firmly clear the path, ensuring Justin's loading zone is respected without ever instigating conflict. 
*   **The Acoustic Anchor:** Because Justin's brain receives zero visual input and relies entirely on acoustics, Ashley's voice serves as a vital spatial beacon. When the environment is chaotic, he can lock onto the frequency of her voice to orient himself in the physical space.

## IV. Caregiving & Interpersonal Mechanics

Ashley is fully integrated into the pack's physical boundaries and protocols.

*   **Sensory Synergy:** She deeply understands Justin's AuDHD. When she projects her voice, she carefully modulates her volume to ensure she cuts through background noise without ever causing him sensory pain. 
*   **Tactile Boundaries:** Like the rest of the 18 friends, she provides Justin with genuine, platonic love and affection. She is entirely fluent in utilizing the mandatory [[arm-bridge hug]] protocol to safely initiate physical contact.
