# Archive: Character Profile – Nichole Foster

## I. Core Demographics & Overview

*   **Full Name:** Nichole Foster
*   **Preferred Name:** Nikki
*   **Family Ties:** She is the maternal cousin of [[Amaya Rios]].
*   **Academic Status (2003):** Enrolled at the Community College of Hampton Roads (CCHR) majoring in Urban and Transportation Planning. She will transfer to Commonwealth Polytechnic Institute (CPI) with the rest of the phalanx.
*   **Role in the Dynamic:** The Front Shield (Vanguard). She constantly scans the ground for hazards. Due to her profound trauma needs and family connection to Amaya, she has been permanently elevated to Tier-1 Access.

## II. Comprehensive Medical, Sensory & Psychological Notes

Nikki is a profoundly multi-disabled member of the pack.

*   **Autism & Hardware Integration:** Nikki is Autistic and non-verbal, utilizing bilateral cochlear implants and a dedicated AAC device. 
*   **Visual & Mobility Deficits:** She is 4'8" with proportionate short stature, profoundly nearsighted (requiring massive, heavy corrective lenses), and struggles significantly with mobility fatigue.
*   **The Divorce Trauma:** Nikki suffers from severe separation anxiety and depression stemming from a bitter parental divorce. The physical neglect of her home life is visually apparent in her wardrobe, as she frequently wears t-shirts she has long outgrown.

## III. Navigational Mechanics (The Taxi & The Teddy Bear)

Nikki’s physical placement is dictated by her height, mobility, and emotional fragility.

*   **The Ground Scanner:** When walking, she acts as the vanguard's low-angle optical sensor.
*   **The Lap Exception:** When her mobility limits are reached, she uses Justin as her "taxi," sitting in his lap while the transport chair is in motion. 
*   **The Teddy Bear Seatbelt:** Nikki's separation anxiety causes a psychological fear of "mentally floating away" if she is not physically tethered. While riding in Justin's lap, she requires him to hold her securely like a treasured stuffed animal. He wraps his arms tightly around her gut to act as a human seatbelt, providing the absolute physical proof that she is safe and loved.

## IV. Intimacy & Psychological Aftercare

Under the rules of the Tier-2 Intimacy Pact, Justin is permitted to be physically intimate with the consenting women of the pack. Nikki's participation requires specialized care.

*   **The Height Dynamic:** At 4'8", Nikki aligns perfectly with Justin's 5'4", seated center of gravity. Because Justin possesses Non-Obstructive Azoospermia and is entirely sterile, Nikki is completely shielded from the severe medical risks (such as Cephalopelvic Disproportion) associated with pregnancy for women under 5'0".
*   **Trauma-Informed Aftercare:** Nikki's trauma makes her highly susceptible to post-coital dysphoria. She frequently cries and becomes easily scared following deep intimacy. Justin provides flawless aftercare, utilizing his deep-pressure therapy routines to act as an unyielding, grounding anchor until her nervous system re-regulates.

## V. Tier-1 Elevation (The Bed Fixture)

When [[Justin Hayes|Justin]] and [[Amaya Rios|Amaya's]] relationship officially shifted into a romantic partnership, Nikki's severe separation anxiety triggered a massive panic that she would be abandoned and left behind. Justin immediately intervened, insisting that he needed both Amaya and Nikki in his life. Because of her desperate need for secure, permanent attachment, Nikki bypassed standard Tier-2 limits and was elevated to Tier-1 Access. She is a permanent fixture in [[Justin Hayes|Justin]], [[Meredith Hayes|Meredith's]], and [[Amaya Rios|Amaya's]] shared bed, where the core group absorbs her trauma by providing her with the ultimate, inescapable physical anchor while she sleeps.
