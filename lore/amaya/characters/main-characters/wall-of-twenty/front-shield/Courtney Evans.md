# Archive: Character Profile – Courtney Evans

## I. Core Demographics & Overview

*   **Full Name:** Courtney Evans
*   **Academic Status (2003):** Enrolled at the Community College of Hampton Roads (CCHR) majoring in Physical Therapy. She will transfer to Commonwealth Polytechnic Institute (CPI) with the rest of the 21-person phalanx to complete her bachelor's degree.
*   **Role in the Dynamic:** The Front Shield (Vanguard). She is the enforcer of the group; she is entirely unbothered by causing a scene if someone disrespects Justin's personal space.

## II. Physical Presentation & Medical Reality

Courtney’s fierce personality is deeply informed by her own complex medical hardware.

*   **Height:** 5'4" (perfectly matching Justin's height if he could stand). 
*   **Malone Appendicostomy (MACE):** Courtney cannot evacuate her bowels naturally. She utilizes a surgically created tract through her bellybutton (a MACE) to administer antegrade enemas. Because this is highly scheduled, she completely washes her colon clean before leaving the house, giving her zero transit anxiety regarding bowel accidents.
*   **Suprapubic Urinary Catheter:** She requires a permanent urinary catheter. Rather than a urethral route, hers is suprapubic—routed directly through the lower abdomen (just above the pubic bone) into the bladder. During the day, it connects to a collection bag strapped to her calf. She strictly wears wide-leg pants to prevent the tubing from snagging—she absolutely hates skirts. While she wears pants for mechanical safety, she does not care if the bag is concealed and has zero shame if it becomes visible.
*   **Liquid Diet:** She cannot process solid foods, consuming specialized liquid nutrition through a straw. She mathematically paces her fluid intake during transits to ensure her leg bag does not overfill before they reach a restroom.

## III. Navigational Mechanics (The Vanguard)

Courtney walks in the front formation, acting as the absolute social firewall.

*   **The TMI Defense (Social Enforcer):** While Danielle handles physical body-blocking, Courtney handles psychological enforcement. She weaponizes her medical reality with zero shame. If a stranger acts entitled or blocks an ADA ramp, Courtney will hike up her pant leg, point directly to her exposed leg bag, and demand: *"What is that yellow liquid in that bag? It’s pee! Now, are you going to get out of my way or do I need to empty this bag next to your foot?"* This instantly makes aggressors deeply uncomfortable, forcing them to retreat and clearing the path.
*   **The Transit Emergency Protocol:** If the pack gets stranded and her leg bag reaches critical capacity without an accessible bathroom, the Vanguard forms a human shield facing outward. Courtney discreetly executes a sterile, "no-touch" drop to drain her bag into a 32oz wide-mouth Nalgene bottle (pre-treated with medical odor eliminator), drops it in a dry-bag, and they keep moving. 

## IV. Caregiving, Intimacy & Interpersonal Mechanics

Courtney's physical and medical realities seamlessly mesh with Justin's needs, particularly behind closed doors.

*   **The PT Synergy (Spasticity Management):** As a Physical Therapy major, Courtney safely stretches Justin’s limbs to relieve his CP spasticity and prevent painful muscle contractures. During intimacy, she uses this biomechanical knowledge to manage his positioning safely.
*   **The Dining Dynamic:** Justin requires direct, manual intervention to eat. Because Courtney's liquid diet requires no utensils, her hands are completely free. She frequently sits beside him to execute his hand-over-hand feeding protocol, sharing a quiet solidarity over their non-standard dietary routines.
*   **Intimacy Mechanics & The Night Bag Swap:** Courtney’s suprapubic catheter completely bypasses her genitals, leaving them unobstructed for sexual activity. Before intimacy, she disconnects her daytime leg bag and connects the abdominal tube to a long extension routed to a high-capacity "night bag" hanging off the bed frame. This removes the bulky plastic from her leg, granting them total physical freedom.
*   **The Tactile Map:** Because Justin is blind and relies on tactile predictability, he has a precise mental map of her abdomen. He knows the MACE port is at her umbilicus, the suprapubic port is a few inches lower, and the silicone tubing routes across her hip via an adhesive StatLock anchor before dropping off the edge of the mattress.
*   **Explicit Communication:** Justin's AuDHD requires literal truth. Courtney's blunt, uncompromising communication style ensures zero guesswork during intimacy; she explicitly guides his hands and vocalizes exactly where her snag hazards are, creating a completely secure sensory environment.
