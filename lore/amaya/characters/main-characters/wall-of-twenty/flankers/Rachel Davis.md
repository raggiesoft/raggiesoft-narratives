# Archive: Character Profile – Rachel Davis

## I. Core Demographics & Overview

*   **Full Name:** Rachel Davis
*   **Academic Status (2003):** Enrolled at the Community College of Hampton Roads (CCHR) majoring in Biological Sciences on a rigid Pre-Dentistry track. She will transfer to Commonwealth Polytechnic Institute (CPI) with the rest of the 21-person phalanx.
*   **Role in the Dynamic:** The Side Perimeter (Flanker). She is the most academically gifted and driven of the group, and the undisputed master of class notes.

## II. The Academic Powerhouse

Rachel operates on a level of academic intensity that outpaces the rest of the pack. She is fiercely driven to secure her DDS.

*   **The Go-To Archivist:** If anyone in the pack struggles to understand a lecture or misses a class, Rachel is the ultimate safety net. Her notes are legendary—highly structured, meticulously organized, and completely comprehensive.
*   **Screen-Reader Synergy:** Her note-taking obsession directly benefits Justin. Because he relies on a QBraille XL and synthesized audio, badly formatted documents are a nightmare to read. Rachel natively structures her notes using clean, semantic hierarchies, ensuring Justin's screen-reader parses her study materials flawlessly.

## III. The Dental Fascination & ADL Integration

Her fascination with the workings of the mouth translates directly into how she assists with Justin's Activities of Daily Living (ADLs).

*   **Oral Sensory Management:** For an AuDHD individual with Cerebral Palsy, oral hygiene can be a severe sensory and mechanical nightmare. Rachel takes the lead on Justin's daily dental care routines. She sources specific, non-foaming, flavorless toothpastes that do not trigger his sensory aversions.
*   **Spasticity & Biomechanics:** She understands the exact biomechanics of the jaw. When Justin experiences CP spasticity, she knows exactly how to safely and comfortably assist him with brushing and flossing without triggering an involuntary bite reflex or causing him jaw pain.

## IV. The Ethical Firewall (Conflict of Interest)

Despite her intense dedication to dentistry, Rachel maintains absolute professional boundaries regarding the pack's medical care.

*   **No Clinical Treatment:** Along with the pack's future MD and LPN, Rachel cites a strict medical conflict of interest regarding the group. She will absolutely handle daily ADL dentistry (brushing, flossing, and mouthwash), but she strictly refuses to ever perform clinical procedures (such as 6-month cleanings, cavity fillings, or x-rays) on Justin or anyone in the pack. 
*   **Clinical Objectivity:** This boundary ensures she maintains her ethical integrity and objectivity, separating her deeply personal caregiving from her future professional dental license.

## V. Navigational Mechanics (The Flanker)

Rachel walks on the immediate left or right of the transport chair.

*   **Calculated Movement:** Her hyper-analytical brain translates well to physical space. As a Flanker, she anticipates crowds. She reads the flow of hallway traffic like an anatomical diagram, naturally adjusting her walking speed and shoulder positioning to seamlessly block incoming students before they get close to Justin’s chair.
*   **The Clinical Buffer:** Because she is deeply focused on her academics, she often walks while mentally reviewing notes or quietly discussing coursework with Justin. This clinical, focused energy discourages strangers from interrupting.

## VI. Caregiving & Interpersonal Mechanics

*   **Tactile Boundaries:** She is completely fluent in the pack's physical boundaries. She utilizes the mandatory arm-bridge hug protocol to safely initiate physical contact, always offering a structured, predictable physical presence.
*   **Explicit Communication:** Her scientific mind appreciates Justin's need for literal, factual truth. She communicates her boundaries and observations with clinical precision, totally devoid of confusing subtext.
