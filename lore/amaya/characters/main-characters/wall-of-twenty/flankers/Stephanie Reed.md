# Archive: Character Profile – Stephanie Reed

## I. Core Demographics & Overview

*   **Full Name:** Stephanie Reed
*   **Academic Status (2003):** Enrolled at the Community College of Hampton Roads (CCHR) majoring in Pre-Law/Political Science. She will transfer to Commonwealth Polytechnic Institute (CPI) with the rest of the 21-person phalanx.
*   **Role in the Dynamic:** The Side Perimeter (Flanker). She is the legal hammer of the group, fiercely defending the pack's civil and educational rights.

## II. Medical & Physical Reality

Stephanie possesses a highly complex physical and neurological profile that completely shapes how she interacts with the world.

*   **100% Silent (Non-Verbal):** Stephanie cannot vocalize at all. She is completely, profoundly silent and relies exclusively on a dedicated AAC device to communicate.
*   **Bell's Palsy & Cerebral Palsy:** She has Cerebral Palsy which affects her motor control, alongside Bell's Palsy, which causes facial muscle weakness/paralysis. Because she cannot speak and has limited facial expressions, strangers often completely misread her intellect.
*   **AuDHD:** Like Justin, she navigates the world with Autism and ADHD. 
*   **Mobility Hardware:** She uses forearm crutches to walk. In sharp contrast to Meredith (who hates her crutches), Stephanie absolutely loves hers. She views them as empowering tools that grant her independence and stability.
*   **Physical Footprint & Trauma History:** Stephanie is 5'7" and medically obese. Her weight is tied to a severe bout of childhood depression stemming from her parents' bitter, combative divorce, which led her to use food as a coping mechanism. Despite strict diet and exercise, she actively struggles to shed the weight. 

## III. Navigational Mechanics (The Flanker)

Stephanie walks on the immediate left or right of the transport chair, utilizing her unique physical footprint to protect the perimeter.

*   **The Crutch Barrier:** As a Flanker, her forearm crutches act as structural extensions of the chair's perimeter. The rhythmic, mechanical planting of her crutches on the linoleum creates an auditory and physical boundary that perfectly deters people from cutting into Justin's space.
*   **Physical Presence:** At 5'7" with a heavier build and crutches angled outward, she naturally commands a wide path in the hallways, effortlessly acting as a physical bulwark against crowded college corridors.

## IV. Psychological & Interpersonal Mechanics

Because of her AuDHD, absolute silence, and visual impairment to facial cues, Stephanie processes the world almost entirely through physical touch.

*   **Boundary Struggles:** Stephanie deeply struggles to understand the concept of personal physical boundaries. She is highly touch-seeking and will frequently initiate contact without warning. 
*   **The Pack's Accommodation:** Within the Wall of 20, this boundary struggle is completely accepted. When she needs to be cuddled for regulation, the pack universally provides it, giving her a safe harbor for her tactile needs. However, the group actively works together to script, model, and teach her appropriate physical boundaries when dealing with strangers or professors.

## V. The Legal Hammer (ADA Enforcement)

Stephanie is currently laying the groundwork to become a heavily credentialed, highly successful Disability Rights Lawyer. 

*   **Braille Advocacy:** If a professor, administrator, or disability services counselor attempts to deny Justin his legally mandated Braille materials, Stephanie is the one who steps in. 
*   **The Silent Threat:** There is nothing more intimidating to an ableist administrator than a 5'7", physically imposing woman with Bell's Palsy planting her forearm crutches, staring them dead in the eye with zero facial expression, and typing out a flawless, legally binding threat of Title II ADA litigation on an AAC device. 
*   **Future Trajectory:** She will eventually go on to achieve massive success as an attorney. Achieving this level of high-stakes, meaningful success fighting for her community will eventually provide the psychological breakthrough she needs to overcome her childhood trauma and conquer her lifelong battle with obesity.
