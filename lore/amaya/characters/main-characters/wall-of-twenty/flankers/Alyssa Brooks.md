# Archive: Character Profile – Alyssa Brooks

## I. Core Demographics & Overview

*   **Full Name:** Alyssa Brooks
*   **Academic Status (2003):** Enrolled at the Community College of Hampton Roads (CCHR) majoring in Parks and Recreation Management. She will transfer to Commonwealth Polytechnic Institute (CPI) with the rest of the 21-person phalanx.
*   **Role in the Dynamic:** The Side Perimeter (Flanker). She is the "Social Shield," an expert at intercepting strangers or classmates who ask intrusive questions about Justin. 

## II. The Municipal Aquatics Background

Alyssa's behavior in the pack is entirely informed by her professional background in municipal recreation. 

*   **The Instructor Trainer:** She works at a local municipal rec center as a lifeguard and swim instructor, and is currently actively training to become a Water Safety Instructor Trainer (WSIT). Because she teaches the people who teach the classes, she possesses elite communication and leadership skills.
*   **The 10/20 Scan Rule:** Lifeguards are trained to scan their entire zone every 10 seconds and be able to reach a victim in 20. Alyssa naturally applies this visual scanning technique to the college hallways, constantly sweeping the periphery for people approaching the pack.
*   **Future Trajectory:** She will eventually climb the municipal ladder to become the Head of the Aquatics Department for an entire city recreation system.

## III. Navigational Mechanics (The Social Shield)

Alyssa operates on the immediate left or right flank of the transport chair, acting as the primary verbal interceptor. 

*   **The Polite Deflection:** When strangers or classmates approach the flank to ask Justin highly intrusive, ableist questions (e.g., "What's wrong with him?" or "Can he understand me?"), Alyssa steps in seamlessly. She utilizes a highly practiced, perfectly polite "customer service" voice to redirect the conversation: *"Oh, we're actually late for a lecture right now, but have a great day!"* 
*   **The Escalation Protocol (Blowing the Whistle):** She acts as the foil to [[Courtney Evans]]. Courtney starts at a ten; Alyssa starts at a two. However, if a stranger ignores Alyssa's polite deflection, pushes the issue, or if she notices Justin becoming sensorially overwhelmed by the intrusion, Alyssa's demeanor snaps instantly. She drops the polite facade and becomes ruthlessly, loudly authoritative—deploying the exact same tone she uses to eject a hostile patron from a municipal pool deck.

## IV. Caregiving & Interpersonal Mechanics

*   **Preventative Burnout Management:** Justin’s AuDHD means processing social interactions—especially unpredictable ones with strangers—drains his "social battery" exponentially fast. By intercepting these interactions before Justin even has to process them, Alyssa preserves his executive function for actual academics. 
*   **Tactile Boundaries:** She is completely fluent in the mandatory arm-bridge hug protocol. Because she is a swim instructor used to guiding bodies safely through water, her physical intercepts when Justin reaches out are incredibly steady, fluid, and predictable. 
*   **Explicit Communication:** Her WSIT training requires her to give clear, unambiguous directions that cannot be misunderstood over the echoing noise of an indoor pool. She uses this exact same clear, literal communication style with Justin, which perfectly satisfies his autistic need for factual, subtext-free dialogue.
