# Archive: Character Profile – Brittany Torres

## I. Core Demographics & Overview

*   **Full Name:** Brittany Torres
*   **Academic Status (2003):** Enrolled at the Community College of Hampton Roads (CCHR) majoring in Electrical and Audio Engineering. She will transfer to Commonwealth Polytechnic Institute (CPI) with the rest of the 21-person phalanx to complete her bachelor's degree.
*   **Role in the Dynamic:** The Side Perimeter (Flanker). She is highly attuned to Justin’s AuDHD sensory needs, often carrying extra noise-canceling gear or backups for his screen-reader earbud.

## II. Physical Presentation & Capability

*   **Height & Build:** 5'5". Brittany is admittedly overweight and actively struggles with weight loss, finding it incredibly difficult to shed the pounds despite her efforts. 
*   **The Flank Buffer:** Her physical reality is a massive tactical advantage for her role in the pack. Her wider, solid frame naturally occupies a larger spatial footprint. When she walks beside the transport chair, she creates a highly effective, physically unyielding barrier that effortlessly prevents crowds from bumping the rear wheels. 

## III. The Micro-Hardware Architect

While [[Lauren Bennett]] handles the heavy IT hardware and structural mounts, Brittany is the master of the micro-electrical grid. 

*   **Breadboards & Assembly:** Brittany operates at the absolute lowest level of computing logic. She builds custom electrical contraptions on bare breadboards, possessing the rare, legendary skill of programming the iconic 6502 microprocessor manually in Assembly language. 
*   **Custom Auditory Hardware:** Because Justin's entire reality is processed through sound, Brittany actively maintains his acoustic hardware. She doesn't just replace his screen-reader earbuds; she manually solders custom circuits, repairs frayed wiring, and calibrates impedance to ensure the QBraille XL's synthesized voice never distorts or causes him sensory pain. 

## IV. Navigational Mechanics (The Flanker)

Brittany walks on the immediate left or right of the transport chair. 

*   **Sensory Overwatch:** Transit environments are notoriously harsh on audio equipment. Brittany acts as Justin’s localized quartermaster. She always carries calibrated backups of his specific, single-earbud setup, alongside custom noise-canceling gear. If his hardware fails or the environment spikes to an unsafe decibel level, she can deploy a replacement instantly without breaking stride.
*   **The Tactile Shield:** She actively protects Justin's physical space. She easily intercepts strangers who attempt to reach into the chair's perimeter to touch him or his equipment uninvited.

## V. Caregiving & Interpersonal Mechanics

Brittany's deep understanding of electrical flow translates into a profound respect for Justin's neurological processing.

*   **Tactile Boundaries:** She is completely fluent in the pack's physical boundaries. She utilizes the mandatory arm-bridge hug protocol to safely initiate physical contact.
*   **Explicit Communication:** Because her brain is wired for the uncompromising logic of Assembly language and circuitry, she naturally communicates with Justin using the exact, explicit verbal consent his AuDHD requires, leaving absolutely no room for implied context or confusion.
