# Archive: Character Profile – Elizabeth Parker

## I. Core Demographics & Overview

*   **Full Name:** Elizabeth Parker
*   **Preferred Name:** Liz
*   **Academic Status (2003):** Enrolled at the Community College of Hampton Roads (CCHR) majoring in Pre-Med. She will transfer to Commonwealth Polytechnic Institute (CPI) with the rest of the 21-person phalanx.
*   **Role in the Dynamic:** The Rear Guard & Logistics. She is the undisputed master of transit logistics and the pack's resident medical compass.

## II. Transit Logistics & Navigational Mechanics

Liz operates in the Rear Guard, stationed behind Justin's transport chair. From this vantage point, she can see the entire formation and direct their path.

*   **The Transit Master:** Liz holds a mental database of every single municipal bus schedule, ADA transfer point, and accessible elevator on the CCHR and CPI campuses. If a bus breaks down or an elevator is out of service, Liz instantly recalculates a new, accessible route without missing a beat.
*   **Patience & Pacing:** Standing at 5'7", she matches the physical flow of the group perfectly. She possesses an immense reserve of patience, specifically with Justin. When his AuDHD or physical limitations slow the group down, Liz never rushes him, systematically adjusting her transit timelines to accommodate his needs without stress.

## III. Medical Ethics & The "Conflict of Interest" Boundary

Liz’s role as the pack's future MD is defined by her strict adherence to medical ethics.

*   **The Clinical Line:** While she fully intends to become a Primary Care Physician (PCP), Liz has permanently stated she will never be the official PCP for Justin or anyone else in the Wall of 20. She cites severe conflict-of-interest concerns, knowing that her deep emotional attachment to the pack would compromise her clinical objectivity.
*   **The Medical Translator:** Even though she refuses to write their prescriptions or act as their official doctor, she is heavily relied upon for medical advice. She acts as their advocate, breaking down complex medical jargon from external doctors, advising them on when a symptom requires an ER visit versus a standard check-up, and ensuring Justin's complex web of specialists communicates properly.

## IV. Caregiving & Interpersonal Mechanics

*   **Tactile Boundaries:** She is completely fluent in the mandatory arm-bridge hug protocol to safely initiate physical contact with Justin.
*   **Explicit Communication:** As a future physician, Liz naturally speaks with clinical clarity. She gives Justin explicit, step-by-step updates on transit routes and schedules, completely fulfilling his autistic need for predictability and logical order.
