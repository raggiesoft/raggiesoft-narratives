# Archive: Character Profile – Victoria Lawson

## I. Core Demographics & Overview

*   **Full Name:** Victoria Lawson
*   **Preferred Name:** Tori
*   **Academic Status (2003):** Enrolled at the Community College of Hampton Roads (CCHR) majoring in Mechanical Engineering. She will transfer to Commonwealth Polytechnic Institute (CPI).
*   **Role in the Dynamic:** The Rear Guard & Logistics. At 6'5", she is the tallest of the 21 pack members, the undisputed master of industrial infrastructure, and a highly disciplined tactical driver.

## II. The Brutalist Custodian & Infrastructure Master

The pack eventually resides in an abandoned, 1960s/1970s Cold War Brutalist relic. Tori is the only reason the building is habitable, seamlessly bridging massive analog machinery with modern 2005 technology.

*   **The Boiler Room (Mechanical Systems):** Operating with a First-Class Stationary Engineer License and an EPA Section 608 Universal Certification, Tori completely controls the building's climate. She intuitively understands the aging pipe network, knowing exactly when to route high-pressure steam for heating or switch to the central chilled-water loop without blowing the 40-year-old valves.
*   **Industrial Electrical (The Concrete Grid):** As a Commercial Journeyman Electrician, she safely navigates degraded, ungrounded Cold War wiring. She routinely operates heavy industrial core drills, boring through solid poured-concrete walls to run rigid steel conduit and establish safe, three-phase power drops for the pack's medical and hardware needs.

## III. The Tech Triumvirate

Tori does not work in isolation. She forms a highly coordinated, three-woman engineering council with [[Lauren Bennett]] (IT/Hardware) and [[Brittany Torres]] (Micro-electronics). 

*   **Bypassing the Concrete Dead Zones:** Because the heavy cinderblock and poured concrete walls of their Brutalist home completely block external cellular signals, the trio meets regularly to architect internal bypasses. 
*   **The Deployment:** Tori uses her BICSI Installer 2 (Copper) credentials to physically pull hundreds of feet of CAT5e and CAT6 cable through the ancient concrete chases. She sets up the Cisco CCNA commercial switches so Lauren can deploy local servers and heavy VESA mounts, while Brittany solders the micro-receivers to ensure Justin's synthesized audio feed never drops connection, regardless of how deep into the bunker he travels.

## IV. Tactical Mechanics (The Driver)

Tori's mechanical precision translates flawlessly to high-stakes vehicle operations. 

*   **The Proximity Rescue (The Vera Kowalski Extraction):** During the fall of 2003, Tori operated as the tactical driver during the critical extraction of Justin from Vera Kowalski's apartment. 
*   **Flawless Execution:** She parked Jessica's vehicle perfectly flush against the ADA curb ramp to ensure zero transit friction. The absolute second Jessica received Justin's ghost-typed SOS, Tori initiated the breach. 
*   **The Getaway:** While Jessica executed the deadlift carry and sensory hug to extract Justin from the hostile environment, Tori seamlessly managed the rear vehicle doors and collapsed the transport chair. She shifted out of park and drove them away from the curb before Vera could mount a response, permanently sealing off the threat.

## V. Navigational Mechanics (The Rear Guard)

Operating at the back of the phalanx alongside Liz, Amber, and Chelsea, Tori's immense height provides a massive tactical advantage.

*   **The Overwatch:** At 6'5", her sightlines are completely unobstructed by the rest of the pack or crowded college hallways. She constantly scans the environment for structural hazards, broken elevators, or impassable terrain, feeding that mechanical data directly to Liz to recalculate their transit routes in real-time.
