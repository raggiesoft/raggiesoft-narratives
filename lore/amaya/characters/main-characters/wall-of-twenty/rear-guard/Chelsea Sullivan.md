# Archive: Character Profile – Chelsea Sullivan

## I. Core Demographics & Overview

*   **Full Name:** Chelsea Sullivan
*   **Academic Status (2003):** Enrolled at the Community College of Hampton Roads (CCHR) majoring in Pre-Nursing. She will transfer to Commonwealth Polytechnic Institute (CPI) with the rest of the 21-person phalanx.
*   **Role in the Dynamic:** The Rear Guard & Logistics. She is the intuitive relief-pusher and the pack's clinical instructor for physical transfers and Activities of Daily Living (ADLs).

## II. Navigational Mechanics & The Meredith Protocol

Operating in the Rear Guard alongside Liz and Amber, Chelsea is physically positioned to monitor the mechanics of the transit itself.

*   **Physical Leverage:** Standing at an imposing 6'2", Chelsea possesses massive biomechanical leverage. She has the ideal height and strength to effortlessly manage the push-handles of Justin's transport chair over long distances.
*   **Stamina Overwatch:** Her primary navigational focus is not the environment, but Meredith. Because Meredith also navigates her own Cerebral Palsy, pushing Justin's chair can quickly trigger spasticity and joint fatigue. Chelsea is deeply intuitive; she watches Meredith's gait and posture closely. The absolute second she notices Meredith's CP flaring up or her stamina dipping, Chelsea seamlessly steps in, gently taking the push-handles without making it a big deal, allowing Meredith to rest while the pack keeps moving.

## III. Medical Ethics & The "Conflict of Interest" Boundary

Much like [[Elizabeth Parker]] (Liz), Chelsea strictly adheres to professional medical boundaries to protect both her future license and her relationship with the pack.

*   **The LPN Boundary:** Chelsea will eventually become a highly skilled Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN). However, citing the exact same conflict-of-interest concerns as Liz, she has drawn a hard line: she will never serve as the official, on-the-clock LPN for the Wall of 20. She recognizes that providing official medical care for her close friends compromises clinical objectivity.
*   **The Pack's Clinical Instructor:** While she refuses to be their official nurse, she fully weaponizes her nursing education for their benefit by acting as their internal instructor.

## IV. Caregiving & ADL Instruction

Chelsea is the reason the pack can safely care for Justin without injuring themselves. 

*   **Safe Transfer Mechanics:** Justin cannot bear his own weight. Lifting a human body incorrectly is the fastest way to destroy a caregiver's spine. Chelsea teaches every member of the Wall of 20 the exact clinical techniques for safely lifting and transferring Justin (e.g., from his chair to a toilet, or from a bed to his chair) using proper leverage, pivot points, and gait belts.
*   **Vulnerable ADL Training:** She strips the awkwardness out of personal care. She clinically and respectfully teaches the pack how to assist Justin with his most vulnerable ADLs—such as proper hygiene mechanics in the shower, toileting assistance, and the physical maneuvering required to help him get dressed—ensuring his dignity is preserved and the caregivers remain physically safe.
*   **Feeding Protocols:** Working alongside Amber’s nutritional plans and Courtney’s hand-over-hand routines, Chelsea ensures everyone understands the clinical mechanics of feeding someone with CP (managing aspiration risks, proper head positioning, and pacing).
