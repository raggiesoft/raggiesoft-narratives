# Archive: Character Profile – Amaya Rios

## I. Core Demographics & Overview

*   **Full Name:** Amaya Rios
*   **Age:** 19 (during the primary 2003 timeline).
*   **Family Ties:** She is the maternal cousin of [[Nichole Foster]].
*   **Academic Status:** Enrolled at the Community College of Hampton Roads (CCHR) majoring in Pre-Veterinary Medicine. She is the standard age for her grade level.
*   **Integration Timeline:** Amaya’s family moved into the adjoining unit of the Hayes family’s duplex during the summer before 5th grade. She has shared a wall, a childhood, and an entire life with the siblings for nearly a decade.
*   **Role in the Dynamic:** The Grounding Force. She is Justin's best friend and adult partner, serving as the ultimate external firewall for the trio.

## II. Physical Presentation

*   **Height:** 5'6".
*   **Physicality & Positioning:** She physically bridges the gap between Justin (5'4") and Meredith (5'8"). When navigating public spaces, she occupies the immediate flank next to Justin's transport chair, providing a secondary tactile anchor point.
*   **Demeanor:** Unapologetic, direct, and fiercely loyal. She observes the world with a high degree of emotional intelligence.

## III. Comprehensive Medical Notes

Amaya’s medical reality perfectly complements the trio's sensory dynamic. 

*   **Profound Hearing Impairment:** Without her hardware, Amaya is profoundly deaf. She lives in total silence.
*   **Sensory Synergy:** Because Justin processes the world entirely through sound and Amaya relies entirely on hardware to access sound, their dynamic requires immense mutual respect for sensory environments. 
*   **The AAC Interface:** She utilizes bilateral external cochlear implants. Unlike many who attempt to hide their hearing hardware, Amaya wears her processors and cables openly, treating the external components as a natural, unapologetic extension of her body.

## IV. The Veterinary Profession & Ethical Override

Amaya's profound visual observation skills make her a master diagnostician for exotic animals. 

*   **The Veterinary Exception:** While the MD and LPN in the pack are bound by human medical ethics that prohibit treating family members, Amaya operates under veterinary ethical guidelines. She is explicitly permitted to treat animals belonging to herself or her chosen family.
*   **The Exotics Monopoly:** Because exotic animal specialists are incredibly rare, Amaya often stands as the sole qualified practitioner for miles. If the pack acquires an exotic pet, her ethical mandate to prevent animal suffering completely overrides any perceived conflict of interest, making her the animal's permanent, undisputed physician. 

## V. Navigational & Social Mechanics

*   **The Intimacy Protocol:** Amaya and Justin share a completely safe, intensely communicative, and fully legal adult relationship. Because Justin has No Light Perception and an AuDHD brain that does not process implied context, Amaya clearly and verbally articulates her consent and desire (e.g., *"Justin, I want to have sex with you"*).
*   **The External Firewall:** Amaya is the immediate neutralizer of outside threats. When abusers like [[Vera Kowalski]] attempt to weaponize false hope or medical gaslighting, Amaya intercepts it instantly—such as loudly diagnosing Vera's fake baby bump as terrible posture to completely shatter the manipulation.
