# The Grounding Exception

## I. The Baseline Stance
*   **The Rule:** In standard Seondu, standing in the ranks requires absolute, statuesque rigidity. Students must stand in *Cha-ryeot* (Attention) or *Junbi* (Ready stance) without touching one another or breaking posture.

## II. The Accommodation
*   **The Sensory Need:** Michael's CP-induced high-tone spasticity and Rachel's severe situational mutism require active nervous system regulation. Physical contact grounds them, calming Michael's muscle spasms and subsiding Rachel's panic.
*   **The Decree:** Recognizing this biological reality, Master April decreed an official, permanent accommodation: Michael and Rachel are permitted to link hands while standing in the ranks.

## III. The Institutional Shield
*   **The Paralysis of Command:** Because April wears the silver thread of the *Sunhoe Sabomnim*, her accommodation is shielded by [[rule-emissary-authority|The Absolute Law of the Emissary]]. Neither Master Ray nor Master Curtis can order the children to separate without directly disobeying a mandate from Seoul. They are forced to watch the siblings hold hands, completely unable to intervene.
