# The Absolute Law of the Emissary

## I. The Voice of the Council
*   **The Proxy:** A *Sunhoe Sabomnim* (Traveling Teacher) does not just relay messages; they are legally and traditionally authorized to speak directly on behalf of the Grandmasters. 
*   **The Weight of the Word:** Within any sanctioned Dojang, their word is absolute law. To disrespect a *Sunhoe Sabomnim* is to disrespect the Supreme Council directly.

## II. The Chain of Command
*   **The Unbreakable Mandate:** An Emissary's decree cannot be debated, questioned, or overturned by any franchise owner (Master Ray) or regional director (Master Scott).
*   **The Only Exception:** Their absolute law can only be countermanded directly and explicitly by Seoul—specifically by the physical presence or written decree of a 7th, 8th, or 9th Dan Grandmaster of the Council, or the 10th Dan Doju.
