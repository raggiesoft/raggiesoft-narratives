## I. The Geopolitical Anomaly
*   **The First Deployment:** This event marks 10th Dan Grandmaster Lee Dong-won's very first trip to the United States. It is astronomically rare for the Doju to personally administer a regional testing floor.
*   **The Federal Coordination:** Because the Seonduwon operates federally as the Ministry of Traditional Seondu Preservation, the trip was a highly classified deployment meticulously arranged by the U.S. Department of State and the Embassy of the Republic of Korea.
*   **The Visa Logistics:** The Grandmaster and his delegation traveled on A-2 Diplomatic Visas, generating significant bureaucratic confusion for State Department clerks unaccustomed to routing diplomats to a light-industrial park in Chesapeake, Virginia.

## II. The DSS Perimeter
*   **The Threat Assessment:** The U.S. Diplomatic Security Service (DSS) assumed full jurisdiction over the Grandmaster's safety on American soil.
*   **The Lockdown:** Local Chesapeake and Virginia State Police executed a rolling lockdown of Professional Place, allowing the federally arranged motorcade of black Lincoln Town Cars to bypass the public entirely. 
*   **The VIP Breach:** The DSS commandeered the right exterior door of the facility, sealing the glass and locking out local politicians and media crews to maintain a strict media blackout.

## III. The Floor Execution
*   **The 7th Dan's Dossier:** Master Scott, operating at the time as a 5th or 6th Dan Regional Director, submitted the highly unusual, personal addendum to Seoul claiming a fifteen-year-old American girl embodied the exact philosophical intent of the text.
*   **The Hijacked Roster:** The delegation hijacked a standard quarterly exam—filled with color belts and junior Pooms—specifically to evaluate fifteen-year-old April under the pressure of a live, mundane environment.
*   **Protocol III:** The absolute moment the 10th Dan stepped onto the gymnastics floor, Protocol III was initiated. Master Scott formally yielded the floor, completely surrendering his regional command to the ultimate authority of Seoul.
