# The Pamyun Recommendation (August - November 1995)

## I. The Pivot (The Hospital Visit)
*   **The Initial Scope:** Prior to Friday, August 18th, Master April was actively drafting a standard disciplinary report regarding Master Curtis's insubordination and Murye (Disrespect) during his failed 3rd Dan examination.
*   **The Paradigm Shift:** Following a phone call from Jessica, April visits the trauma bay and pediatric ICU at the Children's Hospital. The scope of the investigation immediately shatters. Curtis is reclassified from an insubordinate instructor to a violent predator.
*   **Implicating Ownership:** April’s analytical mind connects the structural dots; Curtis could not have committed these atrocities without an enabling environment. Master Ray’s willful negligence, "business-first" mentality, and refusal to monitor his own floor make him entirely complicit.

## II. The Emissary's Petition
*   **The Dossier:** April scraps the initial disciplinary report. She drafts a devastating, comprehensive prosecution of both men. 
*   **The Evidence:** She compiles the medical evidence and translates the local Chesapeake Police reports into formal Korean.
*   **The Recommendation:** As a Sunhoe Sabomnim, April does not have the unilateral authority to erase a practitioner's lineage. Instead, she formally petitions the Grandmasters to permanently strip Master Ray and Master Curtis of their ranks and titles, and completely excommunicate them from the Art. 

## III. The Diplomatic Transit
*   **The Valise Diplomatique:** To ensure the dossier cannot be intercepted, legally subpoenaed, or tampered with by the American legal system or Master Ray's corporate lawyers, April seals the petition inside her Diplomatic Pouch. 
*   **The Protection:** Under international law, this pouch cannot be opened, x-rayed, detained, or searched by TSA, U.S. Customs, or any law enforcement agency.

## IV. The Deliberation & Verdict
*   **The Review:** For three months, the *Choego Pyeong-uihoe* (Supreme Council) in Seoul reviews April's devastating dossier, the medical evidence, and the translated police reports.
*   **The Unanimous Consensus:** In November 1995, the Grandmasters reach a unanimous decision, agreeing entirely with April's recommendation.
*   **The Erasure:** While Ray and Curtis sit in concrete cells in the Chesapeake City Jail awaiting criminal trial, the Seonduwon drops the hammer. A heavy black line is drawn through their names in the international registry, officially and permanently erasing them from the martial arts world.
