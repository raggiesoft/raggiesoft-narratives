*   **The Blue Band:** The OFM (Office of Foreign Missions) color-codes IDs based on immunity levels. The bright, solid blue border grants full, unquestionable criminal and civil immunity.
*   **Law Enforcement Protocol:** Local police possess absolutely zero jurisdiction over the bearer. Any interaction requires the immediate involvement of the U.S. Diplomatic Security Service (DSS).
*   **The Diplomatic Pouch:** The bearer is authorized to transport sealed portfolios that cannot be legally opened, x-rayed, or detained by U.S. Customs or law enforcement under any circumstances.
