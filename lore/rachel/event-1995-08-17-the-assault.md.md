## I. Clinical Overview
On Thursday, August 17, 1995, the standard operations of the 5:30 PM class were maliciously altered. The instructor deviated from established curriculum to utilize unauthorized, high-kinetic static pressure techniques—specifically the Vengeful Variant and Level 3 Crush—on vulnerable and unconsenting students.

## II. The Medical Reality
The application of adult body weight to undeveloped anatomy resulted in immediate, severe trauma. 
*   **Drew (Age 6):** The kinetic force of a standard passing step resulted in a ruptured spleen, requiring emergency surgical intervention at the Children's Hospital.
*   **Rachel (Age 8):** Sustained cracked lower ribs and a bruised spleen due to lateral hydrostatic displacement.
*   **Michael (Age 10):** Experienced a torn abdominal fascia, a severe soft-tissue injury drastically complicated by CP-induced high-tone spasticity.

## III. The Systemic Failure
This event was directly facilitated by the gross negligence of the facility's ownership. The deliberate use of drawn interior blinds and the normalized culture of extreme conditioning (The Iron Shirt Underground) created a controlled environment where adult bystanders were paralyzed by compliance.

## IV. The Institutional Response
The undeniable physical evidence of the assault triggered immediate, mandated reporting protocols by the pediatric trauma surgeons. This medical intervention catalyzed the Chesapeake Police Department's lockdown of the facility and set the foundation for Master April’s excommunication dossier to the Seonduwon.
