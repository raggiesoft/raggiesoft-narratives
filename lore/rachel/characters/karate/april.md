# I. Dual Identity
*   **The Civilian Anchor:** April Mercer is her civilian name. This is the identity on her Virginia driver's license and the name her American students use.
*   **The Bestowed Name:** Upon receiving her emissary credential, her civilian identity was legally and spiritually severed from the testing floor. She was formally adopted into the philosophical bloodline of the Creator and bestowed the name **Lee Hae-jin** (Sea of Truth). 
*   **The Geopolitical Registration:** In the eyes of the South Korean government and international customs, April Mercer does not exist. She is registered exclusively as Lee Hae-jin, a State Agent of the Republic of Korea.

# II. Authority & Credentials
*   **The Rank:** She earned her 1st Poom at age 14, automatically converting to an adult 4th Dan at age 18. 
*   **The Emissary Override:** She holds the highly restricted title of [[cred-sunhoe-sabomnim|Sunhoe Sabomnim (Traveling Teacher)]] (Traveling Teacher). Because she wears the silver thread of the Seonduwon, she possesses absolute administrative authority on any sanctioned testing floor, legally outranking regional directors like Master Scott.
*   **Diplomatic Shield:** Because the Seonduwon operates federally as the Ministry of Traditional Seondu Preservation, April operates under a [[legal-diplomatic-immunity]] protocol. She possesses an A-1 Diplomatic Visa and a blue-banded U.S. Department of State Diplomatic Identification Card. 

# III. The Sanctuary
*   **The Breakaway:** Recognizing the dangerous, toxic shift in Tidewater Tumblers' culture, she established her own independent facility, the [[loc-lynnhaven-academy]].
*   **The Founding Students:** Following the events of [[event-1995-08-17-the-assault]], April officially designated [[char-001-michael]], [[char-002-rachel]], and Drew as the founding students of her new academy.
