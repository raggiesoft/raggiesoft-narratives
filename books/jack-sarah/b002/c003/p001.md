# Part 1: Tent in a Backyard 

The four were gathered in the backyard, and Sarah started pitching the tent. “Just like our childhood, but now we’re older,” she said.

“This is going to be a tight fit, but I’m sure we will manage,” Clare said.

Molly asked, “What are we going to do about changing?”

Sarah said, “Yeah, about that. When I twisted my ankle, Jack helped me with everything. Yes, this means he got to help me get bathed and dressed. So far as I’m concerned, I have nothing to hide from him.”

“Then if you’re ok with it, I’m ok with it,” Molly said, “Jack is this true?”

“Yeah, I helped Sarah during her twisted ankle days, and it doesn’t bother me,” Jack answered.

Clare said, “Then no need for privacy. Probably for the best since this tent is so small.”
