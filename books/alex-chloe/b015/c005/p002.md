# Part 2: A Taste of the Townships

The entire extended family—the Leclercs, Alex, Chloé, and Megan—piled into two minivans for a scenic drive. Their first stop was a local *fromagerie*, a charming little shop famous for its fresh, squeaky cheese curds. They bought several bags, a perfect, authentic Québécois snack for their outing.

Their final destination was a scenic lookout point, a quiet park perched high on a hill overlooking the vast, glittering expanse of Lake Memphremagog, the very view captured in their beloved painting. They spread out blankets on the grass, enjoying the cheese curds and the breathtaking panorama.

As they sat there, a cool breeze rustled through the trees. Chloé, ever prepared, reached into her bag with a mischievous glint in her eye.

"It's a little chilly," she announced, "and I believe two certain Americans still have an outstanding debt to pay." She pulled out not one, but two bright red and white "uniform of shame" toques from their 2010 adventure and held them out to Alex and Megan with a triumphant flourish.

Alex and Megan looked at the toques, then at each other, a slow, evil, and perfectly synchronized grin spreading across both their faces.

"You know, Mason," Megan said, her voice dripping with mock sincerity, "that's very thoughtful. But we came prepared."

Without another word, they both reached into their own backpacks. Alex pulled out a simple, comfortable, black Boston Brawlers beanie. Megan pulled out a nearly identical one. In a single, coordinated act of sartorial defiance, they pulled their Brawlers beanies onto their heads.

Chloé stared at them, at the two proud, unapologetic Brawlers logos, then back at the useless Canadian toques in her hands. She opened her mouth to protest, but then she just... laughed. A full, happy, and completely defeated laugh.

The two uncivilized Americans had won. They had outmaneuvered her in her own country.

She just shook her head, a look of pure, unadulterated love on her face.

IT'S A BEANIE, Alex's device stated, a final, quiet, and absolutely victorious declaration.

Megan then leaned forward, a look of playful, serious concern on her face. "And besides, Chloé," she said, her voice full of mock sincerity, "we're a little worried about you. You were born in Vermont. You're an American. It's really time you started cheering for the correct team."

The entire Leclerc clan erupted in a chorus of good-natured, theatrical boos. Chloé just gasped, a hand flying to her chest in a gesture of pure, mock horror.

"It was an accident of geography!" she declared to the laughing crowd. "IT IS A TOQUE, YOU TWO UNCIVILIZED AMERICANS!"
